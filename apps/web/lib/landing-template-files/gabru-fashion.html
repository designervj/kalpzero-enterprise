Our Story

Rooted in Rajasthan,
Loved Worldwide

Gabru Fashion began with a single shop in the Pink City of Jaipur — a space where artisans and aesthetes could find each other. Today, we bridge centuries of Indian craft with the demands of the contemporary home.

Every piece in our collection is either handcrafted by local artisans or sourced from ethical suppliers who share our belief: that the objects you live with should be made with intention, skill, and care.

FAQ

Questions, Answered

Our team is always here to help you find your perfect piece. For anything else, reach out via live chat.

Do you ship across India?+

Yes, Gabru Fashion ships to all 28 states and 8 union territories across India. Standard delivery takes 5–8 business days. Our Connoisseur and Collector members enjoy expedited shipping within 2–3 business days, with select metro cities receiving next-day delivery.

What is your return and exchange policy?+

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy on all non-customized items. Products must be in their original condition with all packaging intact. Customized or bespoke pieces cannot be returned but may be exchanged for store credit at our discretion. Contact us at returns@gabrufashion.com to initiate a return.

Are your products handmade?+

Many of our pieces are handcrafted by artisans in Jaipur and surrounding Rajasthan villages. Each product listing clearly states whether it is handcrafted, artisan-assisted, or machine-made. We are deeply committed to supporting traditional Indian craft communities and ensure every artisan is paid a living wage.

How do membership plans work?+

Membership plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled anytime. Benefits activate immediately upon signing up. You can upgrade or downgrade your tier at any time; the difference will be prorated. Members also receive exclusive invitations to our seasonal preview events in Jaipur.

Do you offer interior consultation services?+

Yes! Our in-house design team offers virtual and in-person consultations. Curator members receive one complimentary mood board per month, Connoisseur members receive quarterly 1-hour video consultations, and Collector members enjoy monthly home visits from our senior designers. Standalone consultations can also be booked for ₹1,999/hour.
